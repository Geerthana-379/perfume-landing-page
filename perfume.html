About Velosia

Velosia isn’t just about fragrances — it’s about moments, memories, and emotions bottled into an elegant form. We believe that a scent has the power to evoke nostalgia, build confidence, and leave a lasting impression.

Every Velosia perfume is crafted with rare ingredients, traditional techniques, and modern elegance. Our blends are inspired by exotic destinations, timeless romance, and the boldness of individuality.

Founded in Paris, the heart of luxury and style, Velosia is committed to sustainability, cruelty-free processes, and minimalistic beauty. Our design is sleek, our quality is premium, and our passion is evident in every drop.

Join us on this aromatic journey — because you deserve more than just a fragrance, you deserve a signature.
